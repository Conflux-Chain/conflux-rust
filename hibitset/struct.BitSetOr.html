Struct BitSetOr

Source 
pub struct BitSetOr<A: BitSetLike, B: BitSetLike>(pub A, pub B);
Expand description

BitSetOr takes two BitSetLike items, and merges the masks returning a new virtual set, which represents an merged of the two original sets.
